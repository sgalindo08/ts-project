Why Taylor Swift?

Why is Taylor Swift the best?

It"s unusual for an artist to be the music industry. Taylor Swift is a globally famous, acclaimed vocalist while also being an incredible dancer and performer.

Her concerts are well-known for their incredible spectacle - the sets, the choreography - and she sells out stadiums and arenas internationally.

She has sold over 200 million records globally and on Spotify she gained 185 million streams worldwide in 24 hours!
